About this product

Experience the authentic taste of tradition with Traditional Thekua, a beloved delicacy from Bihar and Jharkhand. Popularly known as the “Prasad of Chhath Puja,” this classic treat reflects the warmth of Indian culture, devotion, and togetherness. Made with premium ingredients and sweetened with jaggery, our Elachi Thekua delivers a rich homemade flavor and irresistible crunch in every bite. If you are looking for authentic thekua online, this traditional recipe brings the true essence of homemade Indian goodness right to your doorstep.

Perfect as a tea-time treat, festive delight, or travel snack, this handcrafted Thekua is one of the most loved Indian snacks enjoyed by all age groups.
